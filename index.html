San Francisco 49ers

The San Francisco 49ers are a professional American football team located in the San Francisco Bay Area.
They compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member of the league's National Football Conference (NFC) West division.
The team currently plays its home games at Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara, California, located 45 miles (72 km) southeast of San Francisco in the heart of Silicon Valley.
Since 1988, the 49ers have been headquartered in Santa Clara.
